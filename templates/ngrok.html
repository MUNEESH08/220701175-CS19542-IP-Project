Steps to Install Ngrok:

  1. Download LOCTOGLOB-ng.exe from the LOCTOGLOB webpage.
  2. Download the Ngrok.zip file for your OS and extract ngrok.exe to the same folder as your LOCTOGLOB-ng.exe.
  3. Go to the Ngrok website and sign up or log in.
  4. After logging in, go to Your Authtoken page in Ngrok.
  5. Copy your Authtoken from the page.
  6. Run the LOCTOGLOB-ng.exe file, paste the localhost URL, and press Enter. Then, paste your Authtoken and press Enter.
  7. You will receive your public link.
  8. To close the connection, simply close the terminal or command prompt where you entered the link and Authtoken.
