Steps to Install Serveo:

  1. Ensure that Python is installed on your system.
  2. Download LOCTOGLOB.exe from the Serveo section.
  3. Run the LOCTOGLOB.exe file.
  4. Enter the localhost URL (e.g., 127.0.0.1 or localhost) along with the port number (e.g., 127.0.0.1:5000 or localhost:5000).
  5. Your port will be forwarded globally, and the global link will automatically open in your browser.
  6. To close the connection, simply close the terminal or command prompt where you provided the link.
